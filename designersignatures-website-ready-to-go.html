What is an email signature?

It's the block at the bottom
of every email you send.

Your name. Your job title. Your company logo. Your phone number. Your website. Every professional email ends with one — it's your digital business card, attached to every message, seen by every recipient.

The problem
Most businesses type their signature manually in plain text. It looks inconsistent across devices, images break in Outlook, and there's no team-wide standard.
Our solution
We custom-design a branded HTML email signature for your business — with your logo, brand colours and layout — that looks perfect on every device and email client.
Your whole team
One design, every employee. Each person gets a personalised version with their own name, role and photo — rolled out with one click from your admin dashboard.
